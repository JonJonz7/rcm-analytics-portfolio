Methodology

Opportunities are identified across two categories. Underpayments: paid claims where the payment received falls below the expected contracted rate, calculated as the gap between expected and actual reimbursement. Denial appeals: denied claims with appealable reason codes, where recovery potential is estimated using a reason-code-specific appeal success rate rather than the full billed amount. Confidence tiers reflect historical appeal success rates by denial reason: High (≥50%), Medium (30–49%), Low (<30%). Claims denied for reasons with negligible appeal viability (e.g. patient deductible) are excluded from the opportunity list entirely.
Independent project — analysis built on an individually constructed claims dataset to demonstrate revenue cycle recovery analysis methodology and reporting approach.
